Examples#

These files are provided to assist CGNS users in testing their implementations. They have been submitted by users “as is” and are not certified by anyone other than the sender as being CGNS compliant.

If you have questions about these data files, please reach out to the person listed in the table or post your inquiries on CGNStalk if no contact information is available. Please note that the CGNS Committee cannot provide technical support for the data files listed on this webpage.

We encourage you to share any CGNS data files (along with a graphic file, if possible) or the URL of the data files with the CGNS user community on CGNStalk.

Please be aware that most of the files, and contact information, on this page are quite old.
